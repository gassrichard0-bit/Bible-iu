Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Bible IU ("the App") is a Bible reading application. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Bible IU does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The App operates entirely offline — no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, no tracking, and no internet connection is required for its core functionality.

Local Storage

Any notes, highlights, bookmarks, or reading progress you create are stored exclusively on your device and never leave it. We have no access to this data and cannot retrieve it.

Third Parties

Bible IU contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or data-sharing SDKs. All Bible text data is bundled with the App and requires no external services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.
