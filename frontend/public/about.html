Bible IU

A clean, modern Bible study app

About

Bible IU is a Bible study application that lets you read scripture across multiple translations, mark up verses, take notes, and discuss in small groups — all in one place.

Features

  • Multiple Bible translations side-by-side
  • Verse markup and highlighting
  • Personal and group notes
  • Small group discussions and study rooms
  • Cross-platform (iOS, Android, Web)
